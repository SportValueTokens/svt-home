Performance Payouts

ownership

Ownership of a player token entitles the owner to receive payouts on every game that the player participates in and is proportional to the player score based in its actions.

tokens

Payouts are funded by sale proceeds of new tokens. Tokens are minted weekly, but only within the active season period. Not when there are no games.

price

Traders may want to buy tokens for athletes that perform well, therefore driving price up or sell tokens of non performing athletes.
